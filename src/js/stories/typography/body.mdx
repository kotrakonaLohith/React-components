## Body Copy Spacing

> Body copy by default has no bottom margin on paragraphs. It should be wrapped in a container with the _utility-class_ `c-mb-<32, 20, or 12>` which adds a bottom-margin to direct children.

<article className="c-mb-32">
  <p>
    Body Copy - Open Sans Regular, 16, <a href="#">Inline link normal</a> Sed ut
    perspiciatis unde omnis iste natus error sit voluptatem accusantium. Nemo
    enim quia voluptas sit aspernatur aut odit aut fugit, quias magni dolores
    eos qui ratione voluptatem sequi nesciunt. Neque qui dolorem ipsum quia
    dolor sit amet, consectetur.
  </p>
  <p>
    Sed ut perspiciatis unde omnis iste natus error sit voluptatem accusantium.
    Nemo enim quia voluptas sit aspernatur aut odit aut fugit, quias magni
    dolores eos qui ratione voluptatem sequi nesciunt. Neque qui dolorem ipsum
    quia dolor sit amet, consectetur.
  </p>
</article>
